ABOUT US

Welcome to Verify, your trusted source for debunking misinformation and combating the spread of fake news. Our dedicated team of four passionate individuals from NIT Kurukshetra has come together to address the critical issue of false information in today’s digital age.

Our Mission

At Verify, we believe that accurate information is the cornerstone of a well-informed society. Our mission is to empower users by providing reliable tools to verify news stories, fact-check claims, and separate truth from fiction. By promoting media literacy and critical thinking, we aim to create a safer online environment for everyone.
